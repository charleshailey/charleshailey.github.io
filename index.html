The Work

A Campaign Built Around Intensity

Launch campaign for the Reebok Zigurine training sneaker, featuring Conor McGregor. The campaign delivered a cinematic hero film supported by digital assets — designed to translate the mindset and intensity of elite athletes into a high-energy product launch.

Distributed across broadcast, digital, and social platforms, the work extended the brand's reach at every touchpoint from television to paid media to organic social.

The Challenge

Launching Into a Competitive Category

Launch a new performance sneaker in a competitive training category while authentically leveraging the influence of one of the most recognizable athletes in sports. The campaign needed to deliver a high-impact narrative that would resonate across television, digital media, and social platforms without feeling like a conventional product ad.

The Idea

The Moments Before the Spotlight

Instead of a traditional product ad, the campaign focused on the discipline and intensity required to perform at the highest level. The Zigurine became the footwear designed for the moments before the spotlight — the preparation, the grind, the mindset. Show the relentless work that makes greatness possible.

Execution

Production Across Every Platform

Production was executed in partnership with Deutsch NY, requiring coordination across brand, agency, and athlete stakeholders. Key production components included:

Hero Film

Cinematic athlete-driven commercial capturing McGregor's intensity and training mindset for broadcast television.

Multi-Platform Assets

Broadcast cutdowns, digital pre-roll, social content, and paid media assets tailored for each platform.

Athlete Collaboration

Coordination with Conor McGregor's management team for production schedules, approvals, and on-set logistics.

Agency Partnership

Close collaboration with Deutsch NY to align creative vision, production process, and final deliverables.

My Role

Integrated Producer — Pre-Production Through Delivery

As Integrated Producer, I managed the campaign from pre-production through final delivery — keeping brand, agency, and production teams aligned across every phase of the project.

  • Production planning and scheduling across all deliverables
  • Talent coordination with Conor McGregor's management team
  • Agency collaboration and alignment with Deutsch NY
  • Crew and vendor management throughout production
  • Post-production oversight and final asset delivery

Campaign Impact

Performance at Launch

The campaign supported the launch of the Reebok Zigurine training shoe with a high-energy hero film and a full suite of digital assets designed to extend reach across broadcast and social platforms. By centering the story around McGregor's relentless training mindset, the campaign reinforced Reebok's position in the performance training category.

Case Study — Nike Unapologetic
Nike Women

Unapologetic

A social-first campaign concept redefining how female passion in sport is perceived — confronting the double standard and positioning intensity, emotion, and expression as unapologetic strength.

Campaign Objective

The campaign was built to confront one of sport's most persistent double standards: when men show emotion, it's called competitive. When women do the same, it's called a problem. Nike Unapologetic reframes that narrative entirely — turning every criticism leveled at female athletes into proof of their greatness. The goal was to create culturally sharp, platform-native content that sparked real conversation while reinforcing Nike's position as the brand that stands unequivocally behind women in sport.

The Insight

Women in sports are routinely labeled "too loud," "too emotional," "too aggressive" — the same qualities celebrated in men as competitive, driven, and elite. The campaign asks: what if those criticisms are actually proof of greatness?

The Big Idea

UNAPOLOGETIC. Every visual challenges a common narrative and replaces it with truth. Bold, minimal, culturally sharp — the campaign flips the lens and makes intensity the point, not the problem.

Campaign Assets
Asset 01
Unapologetic — Tennis

Emotion at peak intensity. Sweat, scream, power. This hero asset reclaims emotional expression as strength, not weakness — capturing the exact moment the narrative flips from criticism to proof of greatness.

Asset 02
Precision Doesn't Change

Clean, composed, elite control. This asset counters the emotional narrative with precision — showing that women hold themselves to the same standard as anyone, with zero room for asterisks. "Neither do standards."

Asset 03
Too Loud?

A direct confrontation of the most common criticism leveled at female athletes. The oversized "TOO LOUD?" text paired with raw, unfiltered intensity flips the question back on the audience — followed immediately by the answer: UNAPOLOGETIC.

Asset 04
Too Loud — Or Just Winning?

A zoomed emotional release moment that reframes the narrative instantly. The copy lands the punchline — shifting the question from criticism to fact. The athlete's raw expression becomes the evidence, not the problem.

Asset 05
Strong Is Strong

Raw power. No softness filter. This weightlifting asset strips away any qualifier around female strength — "No asterisk." means exactly that. Strength is strength regardless of gender, and this frame says it without debate.

Asset 06
We Got Louder

Collective energy, unity, celebration. "They said calm down. We got louder." This team moment closes the campaign with community power — shifting from individual expression to collective movement. The sport changes, the message doesn't.

Case Study — Business Platinum Social Campaign
American Express

Business Platinum
Social Campaign

Premium lifestyle social content highlighting the travel and business advantages available to American Express Business Platinum Card Members.

Campaign Objective

The campaign was created to increase awareness of several key Business Platinum benefits while reinforcing the card's premium positioning. Each piece of creative focused on communicating value through real-world lifestyle moments tied to travel, hospitality, and business operations. The goal was to present the card not simply as a financial tool, but as a resource that supports the everyday experiences of modern business leaders.

Campaign Assets
Asset 01
$200 Airline Fee Credit

This asset highlights the airline fee credit benefit available to Business Platinum cardmembers. The visual captures a quiet travel moment inside an airport terminal, reinforcing the idea that the card helps make business travel smoother and more rewarding by offsetting incidental airline costs.

Asset 04
Unlock Additional Business Value

This creative focuses on the operational advantages available to Business Platinum cardmembers. The scene shows a collaborative business environment, reinforcing how the card can support growing businesses through financial tools, rewards, and operational benefits.

Asset 06
Global Lounge Collection

This asset focuses on one of the card's most recognized travel benefits: access to the largest global airport lounge network. The visual communicates a calm, premium lounge environment where cardmembers can relax, work, or recharge while traveling.

Asset 07
$200 Hilton Statement Credit

This social asset highlights the Hilton partnership benefit available to Business Platinum cardmembers. The visual places the viewer in a relaxed hospitality environment, emphasizing the convenience and rewards that come with staying at eligible Hilton properties.

Asset 09
Centurion Lounge Experience

This asset highlights the premium travel experience available through American Express Centurion Lounges. The visual reinforces comfort, exclusivity, and the elevated travel lifestyle associated with Business Platinum membership.

This approach allowed each asset to highlight a specific benefit while maintaining a cohesive premium aesthetic across the entire campaign.

Case Study — Pacifico × Snow League
Pacifico × Snow League

Find Your Way
Social Campaign

Social-first content campaign expanding Pacifico beyond beach culture and into the world of snowboarding through a Snow League partnership — placing the brand in new terrain, both physically and culturally.

Campaign Objective

The campaign was created to challenge Pacifico's traditional beach-first identity by placing the brand inside one of the fastest-growing action sports environments in the world. Partnering with Snow League provided access to a highly engaged, youth-driven audience that values authenticity, outdoor culture, and premium experiences. Each piece of content was designed to feel native to the platform and the sport — not branded content dropped into a foreign world, but a brand that genuinely belongs there. The goal was to expand awareness, attract a new consumer segment, and reinforce Pacifico's core positioning around discovery and exploration.

Campaign Assets
Asset 01
Find Your Line

This hero asset centers on Snow League athlete Totsuka, capturing a quiet, focused moment before competition. The text-forward design leads with the campaign's core message — "Find Your Line" — positioning individuality and self-expression as the brand's central idea in this space.

Asset 02
Discover New Terrain

Featuring a Pacifico-branded snowboard against a mountain backdrop, this asset bridges the brand's identity directly with the sport. The yellow board bearing the Pacifico logo is the visual anchor — a bold, confident product integration that feels organic within the environment.

Asset 03
Feel The Elements

Shot from a first-person POV at the top of an alpine descent, this asset communicates total immersion in the environment. The wide open mountain view paired with the rider's board reinforces the feeling of scale, freedom, and exploration that the campaign is built around.

Asset 04
The Journey Up

This chairlift shot during golden hour captures the anticipation that precedes every descent. The cinematic quality of the frame — warm light cutting across cold mountains — reinforces the premium, editorial tone of the campaign and positions the journey itself as part of the brand experience.

Asset 05
Discover Together

Formatted as a native TikTok post, this AI-generated group moment captures a crew of riders heading out together. The social UI elements are intentionally visible, reinforcing the platform-native feel. The asset communicates shared experience — a key emotional driver for both the brand and the audience.

Asset 06
Official Beer Partner

Partnership announcement asset tying Pacifico directly to Snow League as the official beer partner. This broadcast-ready creative was designed for cross-platform promotion and event signage, formalizing the partnership and extending the brand's presence beyond social into live event coverage.
