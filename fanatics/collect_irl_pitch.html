The Concept

How Topps
Secret Works

STEP 01
🃏
The Topps Secret Tease
Two weeks before Fanatics Fest, Topps Digital drops silhouetted mystery figures on social — teased as the Topps Secret series. Copy: "5 Topps Secret cards drop at Fanatics Fest. You'll only see their faces in Topps Digital. Download now." Drives app downloads before the event starts.
STEP 02
🔓
The In-App Reveal
Once downloaded and registered, users see the 5 Topps Secret agents' faces revealed inside the app. Each has a Topps Secret card with their face, a nickname, serial number, and a location hint for where they'll be each day at the Javits Center.
STEP 03
🏃
The Hunt at Fanatics Fest
5 Topps Secret agents are placed throughout the Javits Center. Fans who find them must show their Topps Digital app open and speak the secret daily code — which only updates in-app every 24 hours — to prevent sharing with non-users. Every find is a real-world card pull moment.
STEP 04
📸
The Pull Moment
Fan takes a photo with the agent, speaks the code, and receives a QR code to scan in Topps Digital — unlocking the Topps Secret digital card of that agent. Only 125 of each card exist. The photo becomes proof of the pull. Every interaction is organic social content.
